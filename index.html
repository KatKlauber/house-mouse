Humane Mouse Removal

Is there a
MOUSE
in your
HOUSE?

Simply pick up some humane traps from the store (Home Depot has some!), fill the bait corner with peanut butter, set the traps where you saw the mouse or evidence of a mouse, and wait for sounds indicating it has entered the trap.

Once the mouse is caught, put on some gloves, carry the trap to a safe, wooded location

  1. near water
  2. away from major urban developments
  3. and more than 2 miles away from your home

Once you've found the perfect spot, slide open the top of the trap (with gloves still on), and let little Fievel go.

Too squeamish to pick up the trap and set it free? I'll gladly do it for you!
